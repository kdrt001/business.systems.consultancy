ISO FAQs

Common ISO Certification Questions

The right ISO standard depends on your industry, customer requirements, and organizational goals. ISO 9001 is recommended for most organizations. ISO 14001 is for those managing environmental impact. ISO 45001 is critical for safety-sensitive industries. ISO 27001 is for organizations handling sensitive data. We offer a free consultation to assess your specific needs.
Costs vary based on the ISO standard, organization size, scope, and current readiness. Costs include consultancy fees, internal training, documentation, and certification body fees. We provide a transparent, itemized proposal after conducting an initial assessment. Contact us for a customized cost estimate.
Yes. Integrated management systems (IMS) combining ISO 9001, ISO 14001, and ISO 45001 share a common High Level Structure (HLS), making simultaneous implementation highly efficient. Our consultants are experienced in developing integrated management systems that reduce documentation duplication and audit costs.
ISO certificates are typically valid for 3 years. During this period, your certification body will conduct annual surveillance audits to verify continued compliance. At the end of 3 years, a re-certification audit is required. KDRT provides ongoing support for surveillance and re-certification audits.
