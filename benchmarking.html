Our Methodology

The KDRT Benchmarking Process

A structured, six-phase approach that delivers actionable intelligence at every step of the benchmarking exercise.

1

Scope & Planning

Define the benchmarking objectives, processes to benchmark, KPIs, and benchmarking partners. Establish the project scope, timeline, and data collection methodology in collaboration with your leadership team.

2

Data Collection

Gather quantitative and qualitative data from internal sources, industry databases, peer organizations, and best-in-class performers. Ensure data accuracy, consistency, and comparability across all sources.

3

Analysis & Gap Identification

Perform rigorous data analysis to compare your performance against benchmarks. Quantify performance gaps, identify root causes, and determine enablers of best-in-class performance.

4

Best Practice Review

Document and validate the practices that drive best-in-class performance. Assess applicability and adaptability of these practices to your organizational context and culture.

5

Recommendations & Roadmap

Develop a prioritized improvement roadmap with specific initiatives, targets, KPIs, and implementation timelines. Present findings and recommendations to executive leadership for buy-in.

6

Implementation Support

Provide hands-on support for implementing improvement initiatives, change management, and ongoing performance tracking to ensure sustained progress against targets.

What We Measure

Key Performance Areas

Our benchmarking covers the most critical dimensions of organizational performance to give you a complete picture.

Financial Performance

Revenue growth, profitability, cost efficiency, return on investment, and financial ratios benchmarked against industry peers.

Operational Efficiency

Cycle times, throughput, utilization rates, OEE, defect rates, and process productivity compared to best-in-class performers.

Customer Satisfaction

NPS, CSAT scores, complaint rates, customer retention, and service responsiveness versus industry leaders.

Human Resources

Employee engagement, turnover rates, training investment, productivity per employee, and talent acquisition metrics.

Sustainability & Environment

Carbon emissions, energy consumption, waste generation, water usage, and ESG performance benchmarks.

Safety Performance

Incident frequency rates, lost time injury rates, near-miss reporting, and safety culture maturity assessments.
